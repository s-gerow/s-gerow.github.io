Who am I?

My name is Seth Gerow, I am a junior at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University with a passion for both engineering and physics. In an effort to explore both of the careers that I am interested in I chose to pursue a degree in engineering physics with a specialization in spacecraft systems. Additionally, I am taking courses to fulfil minor courses of study in Astronomy and Astophysics, Applied Mathmatics, and Computer Science. From this expansive coursework I hope to build a broad collection of skills that will make me a a strong applicant to a variety of fields.

This website exists to showcase my progress within my education as well as my career. Within it I hope to expand on my resume for potential employers as well as display my experience through the projects I choose to embark on. This website is being built from the ground up using HTML, CSS, and Java, If you notice any bugs feel free to reach out and let me know as I am always trying to learn and improve my abilities and products.
